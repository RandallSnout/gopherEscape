About Gopher Escape

Gopher Escape is a hidden pathway game. Each level will get more and more complicated and it's up to you to find the way out. The pathway will will show for a second then be barried under dirt. Remember the path to guide you gopher to safety. The exit is mard with the gopher hole mound in the grass.
